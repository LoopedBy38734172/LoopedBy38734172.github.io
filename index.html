Create yarn your way

Mix, squeeze and watch your yarn form! This kit provides all the essentials to make around 5 metres of bio-yarn. Perfect for experimenting with crochet, knitting or craft projects while exploring a sustainable alternative to traditional yarn.

Our Story

We started this project because we wanted to explore a different way of making yarn. One that uses sustainable materials and gives people a chance to experiment without contributing to the large amounts of existing textile waste. Our kits were created to give people a practical, hands-on experience with crafting that’s mindful of materials and thoughtful in approach. It’s not about perfection or following trends — it’s about exploring, learning and making something tangible along the way.
